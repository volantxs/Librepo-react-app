Vaults

Vaults employ an elemental mechanism to generate themes for each book. In this system, when a reader marks a book as 'completed,' it enhances the reader's elemental statistics(progress bar). These statistics not only determine the reader's emotional equilibrium after reading the book but also offer insights into the type of books readers tend to favor, along with a rough idea of their character stats.
