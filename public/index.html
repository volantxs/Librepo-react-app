Contribute

Readers can suggest new books for inclusion, which will be displayed in the relevant vault for all users, making it a user-contributed repo of selected books.

Upon finishing a book, readers can collect difficulty-based XP, akin to quest completion. Each new book added equates to a fresh quest.
